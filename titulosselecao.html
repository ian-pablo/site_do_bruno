His Titles with the National Team

Titles:

FIFA U-20 World Cup, Olympic Gold Medal, Copa América, FIFA World Cup, CONMEBOL–UEFA Cup of Champions (Finalissima),

Season:

2005 (FIFA U-20 World Cup) Title: FIFA U-20 World Cup Highlight: Messi was one of the standout players in the FIFA U-20 World Cup held in the Netherlands. Argentina won the tournament, and Messi was instrumental in their success, scoring key goals throughout the competition. He was also named the best player of the tournament and won the Golden Boot as the top scorer.

2008 (Olympic Gold Medal) Title: Olympic Gold Medal (Beijing 2008) Highlight: Messi was part of Argentina's Olympic team that won the gold medal at the Beijing 2008 Olympics. Although the tournament was for U-23 players, Messi played a key role, scoring decisive goals, including one in the final against Nigeria, helping Argentina secure the gold medal.

2021 (Copa América) Title: Copa América Highlight: After several near-miss finals in international tournaments, Messi finally won a major title with Argentina. In Copa América 2021, held in Brazil, Messi was crucial to Argentina's campaign, scoring goals, providing assists, and leading the team to victory. Argentina defeated Brazil 1-0 in the final, and Messi was named Best Player of the Tournament and also finished as the top scorer.

2022 (FIFA World Cup) Title: FIFA World Cup Highlight: Messi reached the pinnacle of his international career by winning the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar. After a historic campaign, where he was decisive in every match and scored key goals, Messi led Argentina to victory in the final against France. He was named Best Player of the Tournament and received the Golden Ball, completing his legacy with the greatest title in world football.

2022 (CONMEBOL–UEFA Cup of Champions / Finalissima) Title: CONMEBOL–UEFA Cup of Champions (Finalissima) Highlight: In 2022, Argentina won the Finalissima, a tournament between the Copa América champions (Argentina) and the UEFA Euro champions (Italy). Argentina won 3-0, with Messi being one of the stars of the match. While not as prominent as the World Cup or Copa América, this title added another important trophy to his collection with the national team.
