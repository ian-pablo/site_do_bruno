His Titles with the National Team

Titles:

FIFA U-20 World Cup, Olympic Gold Medal, Copa América, FIFA World Cup, CONMEBOL–UEFA Cup of Champions (Finalissima),

Season:

2009 Awards: FIFA World Player of the Year, Ballon d'Or Highlight: In 2009, Messi was recognized as the best player in the world, winning both the FIFA World Player of the Year and the Ballon d'Or for the first time. This followed a spectacular season with Barcelona, where he helped the club win the treble (La Liga, Copa del Rey, and UEFA Champions League)

2010 Awards: FIFA World Player of the Year Highlight: Although Barcelona did not win the treble again, Messi was awarded the FIFA World Player of the Year for the second consecutive time, standing out as the best player in the world during a year of excellent individual performance.

2011 Awards: Ballon d'Or Highlight: In 2011, Messi won his second Ballon d'Or. He had a historic season with Barcelona, winning the UEFA Champions League and La Liga, and was widely recognized for his exceptional skills and contributions to the team’s success.

2012 Awards: Ballon d'Or, FIFA World Player of the Year Highlight: This was a record-breaking season for Messi, who set the record for the most goals scored in a European season. He won the Ballon d'Or for the third consecutive time and was also named FIFA World Player of the Year for the third time, cementing his status as the best player in the world.

2015 Awards: Ballon d'Or Highlight: Messi won the Ballon d'Or for the fifth time after another outstanding season with Barcelona, where he played a crucial role in the team’s treble (La Liga, Copa del Rey, and UEFA Champions League). His performance during the season was once again extraordinary, confirming his dominance in world football.

2019 Awards: Ballon d'Or, Golden Shoe Highlight: Messi won his sixth Ballon d'Or in 2019, after an impressive season with Barcelona, where he was the top scorer of La Liga and helped the team reach the Champions League semifinals. He also won his Golden Shoe as Europe’s top scorer for the season.

2021 Awards: Ballon d'Or, Copa América Golden Boot, Copa América Best Player Highlight: Messi had a memorable 2021, winning the Copa América with Argentina, where he was named Best Player of the Tournament and finished as the top scorer. He also won his seventh Ballon d'Or, recognizing his exceptional year with both Barcelona and Argentina, who finally won a major international trophy.

2023 Awards: Best FIFA Men's Player, Ballon d'Or Highlight: In 2023, Messi was awarded the Best FIFA Men’s Player after leading Argentina to win the 2022 FIFA World Cup. He also won his eighth Ballon d'Or, following another extraordinary year with both the national team and his move to Inter Miami.
