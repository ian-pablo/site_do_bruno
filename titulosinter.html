2023 (Leagues Cup) Titles: Leagues Cup Highlight: Messi arrived at Inter Miami in July 2023 and made an immediate impact. In his first official competition, the Leagues Cup (a tournament between Major League Soccer and Liga MX teams), Messi led the team to the title. He was decisive in several matches, including the final against Nashville SC, where he scored a goal and provided assists, securing the trophy for Inter Miami and winning his first title with the club. His performance was outstanding, showcasing his class and skill in a new setting.

Messi quickly left his mark on Inter Miami, transforming the team and helping them win the first major trophy in their history.
