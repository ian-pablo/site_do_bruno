His Titles at Paris Saint-Germain

3 Titles:

2 Ligue 1 (French League), 1 Trophée des Champions (French Super Cup)

Two Seasons:

2021–22 Titles: Ligue 1 (French League) Highlight: This was Messi's first season with PSG after leaving Barcelona. Although it took some time for him to adapt, Messi contributed with crucial goals and assists, helping PSG secure the Ligue 1 title. The team had a dominant league campaign, clinching the title with several matches to spare.

2022–23 Titles: Ligue 1, Trophée des Champions (French Super Cup) Highlight: In his second season, Messi continued to be an essential part of the team, further adapting to French football. He scored goals and provided key assists, playing a significant role in PSG’s second consecutive Ligue 1 title. Additionally, PSG started the season by winning the Trophée des Champions with a commanding victory over Nantes, in which Messi scored one of the goals, showcasing his decisive impact in crucial matches.

During his two seasons at PSG, Messi added more trophies to his extensive collection and demonstrated his adaptability in a new league, continuing to make an impact at one of Europe’s top clubs.
